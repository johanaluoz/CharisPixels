ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is

Charitza Song

A chinese-dominican queer photographer and mental health professional based in NYC

Photography has been my favorite creative outlet ever since I inherited my older brother's camera at the age of 12. My favorite medium is 35mm film, and the best way to describe my photography would be as multidisciplinary, consisting of street, portrait, concert, anything that catches my eye and/or anything that makes you feel alive.

I graduated with a B.S in psychology from Hunter College, and currently work full time as a case worker for Hamilton-Madison House.

Some of my passions include: queer theory, mental health awareness, pasta, haagen dasz 'coffee' flavored ice cream, poetry, activism, sunsets, and laying in bed with my cat Moon.

Thanks for looking!
